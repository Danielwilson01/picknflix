Pick 'N' Flix - About Us

Pick n’Flix was created by 6 guys who love to watch movies yet found the whole booking experience rather frustrating!

Ever tried to organise a movie night out with a group of friends? Maybe you want to treat your significant other to a movie on your date night? Maybe you just want to watch a specific movie on your own, but have limited time to do so?

We found that most regions in the UK have several brands of Cinema to choose from in their locality, friends then ended up on different sites researching the same movie and arriving at different screen times. We also found that toggling between sites to locate a showing to suit your timeframe was equally irritating.

This led to the birth of Pick n’ Flix, we’re all delighted that you’re here and hope you find the site useful. It is here that you can search for a particular film title by time of showing or location, you can search for Cinemas local to you to see what’s on. You can compare, choose and be taken straight to the showing that suits you best to book. You can get recommendations, create a free account and have a particular location or preferred theatres stored ready for your use. At Pick n’ Flix you are not limited to searching by Cinema brand, we want it to be our mission to make your movie going experience better, more enjoyable and less time consuming.

Welcome to our site, we hope you create an account and continue your movie watching with us.
